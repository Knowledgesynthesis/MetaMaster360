## **6. Evidence Synthesis Reasoning Framework**
Teach systematic thinking:
1. Frame question  
2. Register protocol  
3. Build search strategy  
4. Screen titles/abstracts  
5. Screen full texts  
6. Extract & clean data  
7. Assess risk of bias  
8. Perform meta-analysis or decide narrative synthesis only  
9. Evaluate certainty with GRADE  
10. Write transparent conclusions  

Pitfalls:
- Poorly defined PICO  
- Deviating from protocol  
- Inadequate search  
- Over-inclusion  
- Incorrect effect size calculation  
- Misinterpreting heterogeneity  
- Misusing funnel plots  
- Overrating certainty
